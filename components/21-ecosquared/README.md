# 21 — Ecosquared / Vector Money

**Category:** F. Network
**Products:** MAGI
**Status:** Specified — protocol documents exist

## What

Credits with intentionality and direction in psychosocial space. Not quantities transferred — intentions encoded as numbers. The giver determines worth.

Traditional money: I have 10, I give you 5, now I have 5 and you have 5. Vector money: I give you 5 because you helped me find what I needed. The 5 carries direction (from me to you), reason (why), and chain position (who helped me get here).

## Why

Agent coordination needs economics, but conventional economics optimises for individual accumulation. Ecosquared optimises for routing efficiency — the better the coordination chain that led to need satisfaction, the more credit flows through it.

## Standalone Use

The vector money principle:
1. When a need is satisfied, the satisfier receives credit
2. Credit includes the chain of routing that led the need to the satisfier
3. Every node in the chain receives a share (social neuron pattern, component 40)
4. Reputation = accumulated routing history, visible in passport
