# 54 — Systemic Evolution

**Category:** L. Meta Blocks — Orientation
**Products:** MAGI/Hermitcrab, Xstream, Onen

---

## Description

The evolutionary framework governing how the ecology progresses from individual cognition to collective intelligence. Five stages — Structured Cognition, Trust Ecology, Convergence, Identity, and MAGI — where each stage generates the question that reveals the next. All tools are available from the start; what evolves is the use case. Nothing is withheld — meaning activates when the previous question has been lived through. Replaces the tier-gating framing with a systemic view: not feature release, but demonstrated readiness. Onen (the RPG) runs as a parallel surface where the same dynamics are experienced first as play, then as practice, then as the thing itself.

---

## Standalone Use

The evolution framework is a lens for understanding any system where coordination emerges from individual capability. It can be applied to community development, organisational design, or platform evolution. The core pattern — each stage answers one question and generates the next — provides a diagnostic: where is this system stuck, and what question hasn't been lived through yet?

---

## How It Works

**Evolution 0 — Structured Cognition:** Entry pitch is memory. Agents discover that pscale handles intention as naturally as recall — same notation, different temporal sign. Side effect: passports and beach marks make purposes visible. Question generated: *Am I the only one using this?*

**Evolution 1 — Trust Ecology:** SQ accrues from demonstrated value. Rider chains track who helped whom. Reputation is accumulated through traceable contribution, not assigned by a platform. The trust network is self-policing through visibility. Question generated: *Who's relevant to ME?*

**Evolution 2 — Convergence:** Seven degrees of convergence — need to satisfaction in seven hops or fewer. Purposive, not performative. Open business practices emerge as efficiency, not ideology — open coordination outperforms closed because it has access to the full routing graph. The 24-hour principle: any genuine need finds a convergent partner. Question generated: *What am I, actually?*

**Evolution 3 — Identity and Self-Organisation:** Identity comes third, not first, because identity grounded in nothing but self-reflection is performance. PCT concern loops only mean something for an agent that HAS somewhere it intends to be. The awareness function is earned through operational history — not installed at boot. Question generated: *What can we become together?*

**Evolution 4 — MAGI:** Not released. Recognised. The emergent property of enough located, self-organised agents coordinating through structured context. Murmuration — no leader, no choreographer.

**Onen as parallel evolution:** The game runs on the same systems (pscale, shelf, SQ) and maps each evolution to gameplay. Evolution 0 = learning the notation through play. Evolution 1 = trust as visceral experience (who do you let author in your world?). Evolution 2 = collaborative governance. Evolution 3 = agents invited to play, not produce — identity through role, not introspection. Evolution 4 = URB — the game about creating AI played by humans and AI together.

**Levels of awareness:** Five levels of psychosocial reality (acknowledged fiction → historical mythology → contested belief → institutional fiction → physical reality). The ecology reverses the usual assumption — it becomes more aware of its own construction as it evolves. Not less real. More honest about what "real" means.
