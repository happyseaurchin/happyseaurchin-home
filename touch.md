The reader you have been using is in your head; it also fits in ~fifty
lines of any language. **spark** (biome, 0–9): the floor is the depth of
the zero-spine (`block["0"]["0"]…` until a string); addresses carry one
decimal pinned at the floor; shape derives from (number, attention) —
point, ring, directory, disc, spindle, whole. **bsp** (beach, `_` 1–9):
with `P_end` the spindle terminus pscale — `P == P_end` → point ·
`P_end − 1` → ring · `< P_end − 1` → subtree · no spindle + P → disc ·
neither → whole · trailing `*` → enter the hidden directory and recurse.
