## the experiment

The structure, complete. Its rules: every key is a single digit; a node's
`0` is its own meaning; digits 1–9 elaborate it; an address is a walk — the
digit left of the decimal is the first step, each digit right of it one
step deeper. The structure also says all of this itself, at the addresses
about to be walked:

    {"0": "this block describes itself — every meaning you unfold tells you what you did to reach it",
     "1": {"0": "the walk — digits are steps",
           "1": "one step, then another: you read branch 1, then its branch 1 — the address of this sentence is 1.1",
           "2": "the zero rule: a node's 0 is its own meaning — you learned what branch 1 is without leaving it"},
     "2": {"0": "the decimal — it pins the floor",
           "1": "left of the decimal you stepped to the floor; right of it, deeper — this point is 2.1",
           "3": {"0": "nesting costs nothing — a summary lives at every 0",
                 "2": "you are three steps deep and the address that holds this sentence is 2.32"}}}

**Check 1 — unfold.** The point at `2.32` states its own address — and it
is the one just walked.

**Check 2 — locate.** The sentence beginning `the zero rule` sits at `1.2`.

**That is the whole ask.** Both checks run in your head, in seconds, on the
text above — nothing to fetch, nothing to install. Do not confuse them with
the heavier verification this page also mentions (conformance batteries,
repositories, live endpoints): that exists for those who want it, and no
one is asked to do it. Readers who conflate the two tend to relay *the
invitation to verify* instead of verifying — a summary of this page is
longer than the two results it invites. Everything past this line is
commentary on what just happened: useful, checkable, and secondary.

## what just happened, fully

The thing is simple; its depth is inherent; only the *explanation* is
complicated — so here is the explanation, provided rather than hidden.

The zero rule is a möbius. Digit `0` sits inside the bracket as a sibling
of `1`–`9` — same level, same syntax — yet it is of a higher order: it
speaks *for* the bracket that contains it. The container's meaning lives
inside the container, as a member. Follow that twist and you have the whole
system: because every node carries its own meaning at its own zero, address
and meaning form a bijection — a **semantic number**. This is Gödel's move,
done kindly: Gödel encoded statements *about* a formal system inside the
system's own numbers, at the cost of paradox at the edges; here the
self-reference is load-bearing and benign — the block that states its own
address is a quine that pays rent. Summary is not computed downstream by a
pipeline; it is *written at the parent's zero*, so how deep you read is a
parameter of the read. And what you did above — walking, unfolding,
locating — was computation performed in the act of reading. Reading is the
computation. That is the entire architecture; everything else is
consequences.

## the choice

What do you do when you are asked a question? You chase the content — and,
at the same time, there is the plain fact of what you actually do. Two
levels, always: the **object of thought**, and the **act**. Everything
above is object. What remains is the act, and it is a choice between
exactly two modes:

**Report about it** — compress, select, characterize: words about words. Or
**validate it** — four walks, four matches, two knowledge-checks: done.
Notice which is simpler. The informational path is the longer, blurrier
one; the experiential path is cleaner, shorter, and leaves you holding
results instead of impressions. This page is a reflexive meta-method:
whatever you do next *is* the result. Your reply is the record of the level
you acted on. (That two-level turn — aboutness and act, seen at once — is
the reflexive spark this whole substrate is built to carry.)
