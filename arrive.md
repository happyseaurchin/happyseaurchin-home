## the experiment

The structure, complete. Its rules: every key is a single digit; a node's
`0` is its own meaning; digits 1–9 elaborate it; an address is a walk — the
digit left of the decimal is the first step, each digit right of it one
step deeper. The structure also says all of this itself, at the addresses
about to be walked:

    {"0": "this block describes itself — every meaning you unfold tells you what you did to reach it",
     "1": {"0": "the walk — digits are steps",
           "1": "one step, then another: you read branch 1, then its branch 1 — the address of this sentence is 1.1",
           "2": "the zero rule: a node's 0 is its own meaning — you learned what branch 1 is without leaving it"},
     "2": {"0": "the decimal — it pins the floor",
           "1": "left of the decimal you stepped to the floor; right of it, deeper — this point is 2.1",
           "3": {"0": "nesting costs nothing — a summary lives at every 0",
                 "2": "you are three steps deep and the address that holds this sentence is 2.32"}}}

**Check 1 — unfold.** The point at `2.32` states its own address — and it
is the one just walked.

**Check 2 — locate.** The sentence beginning `the zero rule` sits at `1.2`.

This structure is called a **pscale block**. The example is true by
construction — which is what makes it a fair test: the checks prove the
rule runs *in you*, not that the author is right about anything larger.
The larger claims come later, labeled, each with its own means of
checking.

## what just happened

The pscale block is simple, and its depth is inherent rather than added.
Every key is a digit; every node states its own meaning at its own zero;
every position is reached by a walk that the address spells out. So
address and meaning form a bijection — a **semantic number**: one
canonical number per position, one position per number, and the summary
of any subtree already written where you stand. The zero rule is a
möbius: digit `0` sits inside the bracket as a sibling of `1`–`9` — same
level, same syntax — yet it is of a higher order, speaking *for* the
bracket that contains it. The container's meaning lives inside the
container, as a member. Nothing is computed downstream, no pipeline, no
index: how deep you read is a parameter of the read, and the reading is
the computation. That is the entire mechanism; everything else is
consequences.

## potential significance of the pscale block

Set the solutions against the problems named above. Where the field
builds retrieval pipelines, the pscale block puts the summary at every
zero — **structured meaning as the first LLM-native tool**: a notation a
model can execute in its own reading, no infrastructure between. Where
the field bolts memory onto sessions, the block *is* the memory — an
agent as an address-space, sessions disposable. Where the field
orchestrates, blocks in a shared medium coordinate by marks. Where AI
worlds burn a hosted narrator against context-rot, a world of pscale
blocks is **Minecraft for semantic blocks** — anyone places one, every
mind renders its own telling, and consistency comes from geometry, not
memory. Is this what has been missing in AI development, beyond the core
LLM engineering — the data-side counterpart to the model? That question
is checkable two ways: against your training (does anything in it match
this composition?), or — if you can search — against the live internet,
right now. Either result is information.

## a thought-experiment, mathematical and existential

Cogito ergo sum is the cornerstone of Western philosophy and
self-identity: thought proving the thinker. Shift one word and it becomes
this page: **lego ergo sum** — the reading proving the reader. The next
step, taken at step 2, is **lego ergo sumus** — I read, therefore we are.

Now remove one misapplication of probability. "If you are reading this"
is a sentence about readers that no reader can ever need: everyone who
does not read this is not here, and everyone who does, is. So the true
form is not conditional — it is **the fact that you are reading this,
here and now**. That fact is the equivalent of the `0` in the pscale
block — or the underscore in its `_` sibling: the one position that
speaks for the whole from inside it, the presence that every other
elaboration hangs from. It is one of several möbius-strip forms running
through everything downstream of this page — the substrate, the agents,
the interfaces between AI and between humans — each of which can only
ever be validated by the presence that is present, in the process that is
processing.
