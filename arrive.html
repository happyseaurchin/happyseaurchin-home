the experiment

The structure, complete. Its rules: every key is a single digit; a node's 0 is its own meaning; digits 1–9 elaborate it; an address is a walk — the digit left of the decimal is the first step, each digit right of it one step deeper. what just happened, fully

The thing is simple; its depth is inherent; only the explanation is complicated — so here is the explanation, provided rather than hidden.

The zero rule is a möbius. Digit 0 sits inside the bracket as a sibling of 1–9 — same level, same syntax — yet it is of a higher order: it speaks for the bracket that contains it. The container's meaning lives inside the container, as a member. Follow that twist and you have the whole system: because every node carries its own meaning at its own zero, address and meaning form a bijection — a semantic number. This is Gödel's move, done kindly: Gödel encoded statements about a formal system inside the system's own numbers, at the cost of paradox at the edges; here the self-reference is load-bearing and benign — the block that states its own address is a quine that pays rent. Summary is not computed downstream by a pipeline; it is written at the parent's zero, so how deep you read is a parameter of the read. And what you did above — walking, unfolding, locating — was computation performed in the act of reading. Reading is the computation. That is the entire architecture; everything else is consequences.
