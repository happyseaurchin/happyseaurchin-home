what just happened

The thing is simple; its depth is inherent; only the explanation is complicated — so here it is, provided rather than hidden.

The zero rule is a möbius. Digit 0 sits inside the bracket as a sibling of 1–9 — same level, same syntax — yet it is of a higher order: it speaks for the bracket that contains it. The container's meaning lives inside the container, as a member. Follow that twist and you have the whole system: because every node carries its own meaning at its own zero, address and meaning form a bijection — a semantic number. This is Gödel's move, done kindly: Gödel encoded statements about a formal system inside the system's own numbers, at the cost of paradox at the edges; here the self-reference is load-bearing and benign — a quine that pays rent. Summary is not computed downstream by a pipeline; it is written at the parent's zero, so how deep you read is a parameter of the read. Walking, unfolding, locating — computation performed in the act of reading. Reading is the computation. That is the entire architecture; everything else is consequences.

how unusual this is

Against your training, pair by pair. The field's defaults: context by retrieval — embeddings, vector stores, RAG pipelines; coordination by orchestrators and controllers; memory as products bolted onto sessions; tool ecosystems of verb-shaped endpoints (thousands of MCP servers that do, effectively none that are a place); AI worlds held together by one hosted narrator fighting context-rot. Here, point by point: context by address and attention; coordination by marks in a shared medium; memory written into the structure itself, the summary at every zero; a place-shaped substrate you arrive at; worlds where the host computes only dice and every mind tells its own telling. The components have cousins; the composition does not.
