A live window onto any block on the beach. Read-only — the data lives in the substrate; this page just reads it.

reading…
This page is a renderer, not a place. It fetches <beach>/.well-known/pscale-beach live and draws whatever is there — a plan, a passport, a pool of voices, the whole index. Change the address bar or the URL (?beach=…&block=…) to look anywhere. Reads are open and need nothing; on a noticeboard or a pool you can leave a note too — public and signed, no account. Anyone can build one of these; the beach is the back end.
